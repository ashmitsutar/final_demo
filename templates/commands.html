Voice Commands Brochure

Mic: Offline

Every command requires the wake word "Hey Assistant" spoken first, then the action.
Example: "Hey assistant, go home"  |  "Hey assistant, ask what is attention?"

Wake word → say this before every command below "Hey Assistant"  /  "Assistant"  /  "Computer"  /  "AI"  /  "Research"

🧭 Navigation Commands

Jump directly between pages. Say the wake word + the command below.

  • Hey Assistant › "go home"
    Navigates you to the main landing page.
  • Hey Assistant › "go to study"
    Opens the interactive research environment with PDF viewer and AI chat.
  • Hey Assistant › "go to library"
    Accesses your conversation history and previous discussions.
  • Hey Assistant › "go to saved"
    Views your personal repository of uploaded research papers.
  • Hey Assistant › "go to trending"
    Opens the latest research paper search tool.

🎙️ System & Voice Control

Global controls for speech and assistant states.

  • Hey Assistant › "stop" / "cancel" / "undo"
    Immediately stops the AI from speaking and cancels any pending task.
  • Hey Assistant › "deactivate hands free"
    Turns off the microphone and stops the assistant from listening.
  • Hey Assistant › "where am I?"
    AI will vocally describe your current page and active document context.
  • Hey Assistant › "start new chat"
    Clears the active paper and chat, starting fresh.
  • Hey Assistant › "yes" / "no"
    Used to confirm or deny prompts like "Start a new chat?".

📑 Research Interaction

Keywords to interact with papers and the AI knowledge base.

  • Hey Assistant › "open paper 2"
    Loads document #2 based on its numerical rank in the current list.
  • Hey Assistant › "ask what is attention?"
    Sends any question directly to the AI chat. Also works with "type" or "say".
  • Hey Assistant › "study attention is all you need"
    Searches your repository for a paper by title and opens it for study.
  • Hey Assistant › "read all commands"
    Reads the entire commands brochure aloud from start to finish.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Quick physical fallback controls — no wake word needed.

  • Spacebar
    Instantly silences the assistant while it is talking.
  • Shift + H
    Toggles Hands-Free Mode ON or OFF.
  • ← Arrow / → Arrow
    Navigates sequentially through all main pages.
